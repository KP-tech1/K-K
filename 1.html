My dearest Kimberly, my princess, my love, my pumpkin...

Every moment with you feels like a blessing - from our beach days at Maracas to our cozy nights with Bollywood movies, from enjoying your amazing macaroni pie to sharing ice cream from Bellas. You've shown me what true love means, and I'm forever grateful.

To My Sweet Princess

My dearest Princess,

As I sit down to write this letter, my heart overflows with love for you. Four years have passed since that beautiful October day when our souls connected, and yet it feels like only yesterday that I first saw your smile that would come to light up my world.

You are my everything - my princess, my love, my pumpkin. In your eyes, I found my home. In your laughter, I found my joy. In your embrace, I found my peace.

Like Winnie the Pooh said, "You're braver than you believe, stronger than you seem, and smarter than you think." You've shown me strength, kindness, and wisdom beyond measure, and I'm in awe of the woman you are.

I promise to always be by your side, through sunny beach days and stormy nights. To cherish our love for macaroni pie, cricket matches, and Bollywood movies. To hold your hand as we explore this beautiful world together.

You are the poetry my heart has been trying to write all my life. With every beat, it whispers your name. With every breath, it calls out for you.

Happy four-year anniversary, my love. Here's to countless more years of laughter, adventures, and growing old together.

Forever yours,

Kris
